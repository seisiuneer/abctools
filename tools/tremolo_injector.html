ABC MIDI Program Injector

Injects [I:MIDI=program custom1] before runs of notes longer than an eighth note, and [I:MIDI=program 141] before runs of eighth notes.

Input ABC

Output ABC

Ready.
This utility assumes the note-length comparison is based on the tune's effective unit note length, taken from the most recent L: field. A note is treated as an eighth note when its duration equals the current unit length. Anything longer than that is treated as a long note.
