Apple Spatial Photo → Side-by-Side or 3D Anaglyph JPEG

Open an Apple Spatial Photo HEIC file, extract the two embedded stereo views,
and export a Side-by-Side or 3D Anaglyph JPEG image locally from your browser.

You can also drag and drop a single spatial HEIC file onto this panel.
For stereograph cards, use Side-by-Side JPEG with Left / Right order.  For cross-eye viewing use Side-by-Side JPEG with Right / Left order.
For any of the anaglyph output format options, use Left / Right order.
4096 px

For side-by-side output, each eye is rendered at half the selected output width.   For anaglyph output, the selected width is the full image width.
