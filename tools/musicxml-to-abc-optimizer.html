MusicXML to ABC Optimizer

  • Paste ABC notation into the input area, open one or more .abc or .txt files, or send tunes directly from the ABC Transcription Tools.
  • Select one or both optimization options. The Process ABC button is available only while at least one option is selected.
  • Shorten excessively long sustained notes removes only ties crossing measure lines when retaining the next tied segment would exceed the selected duration. Each tune uses its own Q:, M:, and L: values. When a tie is removed, any accidental required to preserve the sustained pitch is written explicitly on the newly re-attacked note.
  • Convert percussion voices for General MIDI playback detects voices declared with perc or clef=perc, interprets their percussion note mappings using the tune's key signature, and rewrites them as ordinary treble-clef voices using %%MIDI program 128.
  • If a percussion voice contains any I:percmap directives, those mappings are used and %%MIDI drummap directives are treated only as fallback data. If the same source note has conflicting mappings, the I:percmap mapping takes priority and the conflict is reported.
  • General MIDI Level 2 percussion numbers 27 through 87 are accepted. Notes whose selected mapping is outside that range, and percussion notes with no selected mapping, are replaced with rests of the same duration and reported in the status area.
  • Converted percussion notes are written two octaves higher with transpose=-24 to keep the notes near the staff while preserving their General MIDI drum pitches.
  • For converted percussion voices, K:none, MIDI channel 10, old program and percussion-map directives, and stafflines=1 are removed. A voice name containing the complete word automobile is changed to nm="GM Drums", and any existing short voice name is changed to snm="GM Drums". The converted voice inherits the tune's existing key signature.
  • When both options are selected, long-note shortening is performed before percussion remapping.
  • After processing, the status area shows aggregate counts. Use Show Processing Details Report to see the tune index, title, and tune-by-tune counts.
  • Use the output buttons to copy, save, or send the processed ABC directly to the ABC Transcription Tools.
